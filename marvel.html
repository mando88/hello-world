Martin Goodman was an American publisher of pulp magazines, paperback books, men's adventure magazines, and comic books. He also founded the company that would become Marvel Comics. Then later Stan Lee took over marvel comics and continued to create heroes. Stan Lee is a comic-book writer, editor, publisher, media producer, television host, actor and former president and chairman of Marvel Comics. His first comic book character was Captain America and later on he also help create marvels first superhero team The Fantastic Four.
